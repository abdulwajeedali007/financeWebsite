About Us

We are a team of enthusiastic professionals who believe in providing innovative technical solutions that could simplify and enhance the human life.

WHO WE ARE…

Salary Day is an advanced fintech product that offers short-term personal loans to the employed professionals. We’re a group of closely knit fintech experienced personnel who have gathered to work for a common cause – Disrupting the fintech industry with a millennial innovative product who are either new to credit or not considered by the established financial institutions for various reasons.

Through our Salary Day app we intend to provide short-term personal loans to any eligible salaried employee post verifying the KYC, and income and employment documentation.

WE’RE A PRODUCT OF A LISTED PUBLIC LIMITED COMPANY LICENSED BY RBI AS NBFC

YES! You got us right! Goyal Associates Ltd is a registered and licensed NBFC from RBI. The company is a 22-yr old public limited entity which is listed on Bombay Stock Exchange. Goyal Associates Ltd holds the RBI registration to approve and disburse loan products, or to carryout financial businesses. Salary Day is one of the latest products of Goyal Associates Ltd. and is expected to create a reform in the fintech industry with its digital loan process.
