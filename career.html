Credit Manager - Underwriting

Job Description

  • Risk management and portfolio monitoring for personal loans.
  • Analyse and assess various KYC documents and able to comment the credibility of the customer.
  • Improving controllership by way of building strong system.
  • Building and automating credit approval engine.
  • Support launch of new products with policy and system build and implementation.
  • Formulating risk policy and support to launch digital product.

Job Requirements

Qualification – MBA and/or Degree

Job Knowledge and experience –

  • Should have at least 3 years of experience in credit / risk environment.
  • Should have an exposure of working with NBFC.
  • Should have good understanding of consumer documents, KYC documents, etc.
  • Should have good knowledge of reviewing and estimating credit worthiness against to risk.
  • Should be able to drive and work with a team reporting.
